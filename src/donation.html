ABOUT US

The club is chartered by Harvard Business School, but is entirely self funded, based on event revenues, membership fees, and donations and contributions by our generous sponsors. We were founded in 1974 to serve HBS alumni and the Northern California community by providing opportuning for connecting, learning, advancing their careers and/or serving their community. Today we serve around 8K HBS alumni. Please learn more about our activities to support our alumni, local non-profits and startups below. Educational topics we cover include entrepreneurship, startups, leadership, community service, women, under-represented minorities in business, and more.
