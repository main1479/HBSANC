Competitor Eligibility and Rules

  • 1) Application fee: $100 (includes mentoring, networking, and free access to all pre-competition HBS New Venture Competition events, access to numerous in-kind benefits, and a chance to win substantial cash prizes - last year $2,500 and $5,000, respectively)
  • 2) They must be a graduate of any Harvard school
  • 3) They must be a founder and/or full-time member of the startup management team
  • 4) The founder/management team member must be represented at all events
  • 5) The founder must be the pitch lead
  • 6) The company must have raised less than $2.5 million in capital and have lifetime revenues of less than $2 million
  • 7) Startups that have participated in the competition in the past are eligible to compete again
  • 8) The founder/company can reside in any location, worldwide

Judging Criteria

  • 1) Customer value proposition
  • 2) Go-to-market strategy
  • 3) Economic model and team
